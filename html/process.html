THE PROCESS

All our programs are based and rooted in character and leadership development. Our school development program is targeted at developing grassroots football in primary schools. It’s also a platform for discovering talents. This program is for Age 5-12 years; players connect with coaches, participate in drills and skills training and benefit from our character development courses.

Pathway Dreams academy host trials every year in August for U10 – U12 players in Ijebu Ode and we scout for talents around all geo-political zones in Nigeria. Footballers selected after this tough process will be invited into our U10 & U12 academy teams. We are not looking to recruit players age 13 and older.

For players successfully making it through the trial process, the Pathway Dreams Academy offers holistic pathways to exceptional young talents based on education, character and football over a sustained period of not less than seven (7) years. All our programs help us in our goal of seeking to raise a new generation of purpose driven men who will impact our nation.

Please Note: You cannot pay to gain admission into this academy, you have to be scouted.
